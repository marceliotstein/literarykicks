Photo Contributions: We asked members of the LitKicks community to submit photos to help make our party complete. Because our 24 Hour Poem followed the progression of a single day, evening, night and morning, we asked contributing photographers to let us know what time each picture was taken, and presented them accordingly.

The images below were displayed during the poetry party. We regret that we couldn't use every image that was sent to us, but we would like to thank all of our contributors for helping to make this event a great success.
