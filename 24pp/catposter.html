LitKicks Presents ...
a once-in-a-lifetime online event celebrating the 10th anniversary of LitKicks.com, protesting the horribleness of everything, and featuring appearances by special guests
Dear Honored Guest:

Would you please take a few minutes to "donate a question" to the LitKicks 24 HOUR POETRY PARTY?

This real-time event will bring tens of thousands of readers and writers from all over the world together in a riotous burst of poetic collaboration. For 24 hours, we will ask participants to compose spontaneous verse in response to a fast-moving series of questions, koans and multimedia stimuli.

We are showing you this preview page because we would be honored to include a simple contribution from you in our itinerary for this event. Here's what we're looking for:

• a controversial question about a topical issue (e.g. "has war become a form of reality tv?")

• a provocative question of a personal nature ("what is the truest love you have ever felt?")

• a writing challenge (e.g. "write four lines of iambic pentameter describing the room you're in right now")

• an original "koan" (a puzzling utterance or observation designed to produce enlightenment)

• any other type of question, statement, phrase or quotation designed to inspire the poetic spirit

We are hoping to include contributions from notable poets who represent the diversity of today's literary scene, including contemporary academic poetry, hiphop and spoken word, postmodernism and surrealism, beat and underground poetry and the international scene.

It would mean a lot to us if you would send us a few words by email to brooklyn@litkicks.com. Please respond by June 30 if possible. Thank you!


About LitKicks: LitKicks.com has been representing alternative literature on the internet since 1994 with informative articles, discussion boards, creative writing workshops and an innovative advertising program for indie publishers. With over 40,000 pageviews a day, 80,000 unique readers a month and the best search engine rankings of any literary website, we have consistently presented an engaging, lively and interactive experience for people of all ages, backgrounds, nationalities and levels of education who wish to experience the joy of great literature and literary community.
The 24 HOUR POETRY PARTY will take place on LitKicks.com from Friday, July 23 12 noon to Saturday, July 24 12 noon. Participants will be guided through an extensive series of literary challenges, provocations and experiments.
  • Who is invited to participate? You and all your friends.
  • Is writing experience necessary? LitKicks does not believe in writing experience. If you can talk you can write.
  • What do I need to bring? Your brain. High speed connection preferred.
