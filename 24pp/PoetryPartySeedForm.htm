24 Hour Poetry Party

Contribute a “Seed”

 

Please support the LitKicks 24 HOUR POETRY PARTY by contributing a “seed” – a thought, image, idea, quotation or question for other writers to respond to. The 24 HOUR POETRY PARTY is an online poetry event that will begin on Friday, July 23 at 12 noon and continue until Saturday, July 24 at 12 noon.  During that time we will present participants with a variety of writing challenges and exercises, and it would be a great honor to include a contribution from you as an inspiration for one of these exercises. 

 

Your contribution can be in any format, including:

 

  • a question or topic for debate (controversy is okay!)
  • an original koan (a provocative or puzzling utterance)
  • a few lines of verse, or a complete short poem
  • a few lines of fiction or prose
  • a quotation from another writer

 

We also hope to touch on a variety of themes both playful and serious, such as:

 

  • observation (of nature, of society, of modern life)
  • personal history or memory
  • relationships, family, love
  • global conflict, violence, war, peace

 

We would appreciate it very much if you could find the time to send back this form or a separate sheet of paper containing your literary contribution by June 30, 2004.   You can also send e-mail to brooklyn@litkicks.com.  Thank you!
