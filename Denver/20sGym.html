20th Street Gym, 20th and Curtis


``Especially on Wednesday and Saturday nights throughout the remainder of the Thirties
did we consistently meet at the Bath House on 20th and Curtis, a place of shower cleanliness and smut, pool joy and fatigue, gym exertion, shuffleboard and table tennis skill, which the city provided free for such as we...'' [First Third p. 103]
