What's New at LitKicks

Here at LitKicks, we enjoy shaking things up.

We feel we've gone as far as we can go with traditional message boards, and we're now moving in a different direction. We want to use the power of online community to inspire writers to break barriers and reach new levels of creative excellence. And we want to publish the resulting great stuff in books. We're going to experiment with a few different types of online collaborative writing events over the next several months.

The first is called October Earth and we think it's going to be pretty interesting. We hope you'll be a part of it.
