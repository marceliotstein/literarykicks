Action Poetry: Literary Tribes for the Internet Age

Showcasing a diverse range of styles, themes and genres, "Action Poetry: Literary Tribes for the Internet Age" roars forth with the unpredictability and energy of an online flash mob. This anthology of fiction, poetry and experimental writing represents the work of over fifty writers as well as the full text of the astounding instant poem created by over a hundred participants during the LitKicks 24 Hour Poetry Party of July 23-24, 2004.

Action Poetry: Literary Tribes for the Internet Age includes a foreword by David Amram. The book is available on Amazon.com, Powells.com and through Barnes & Noble.
