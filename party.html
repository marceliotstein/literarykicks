THE LITKICKS 24 HOUR POETRY PARTY IS ON!

From now until Saturday 12 noon EST we will be working together to create the greatest group poem ever written, a spontaneous epic describing 24 hours in the crazy, troubled life of our misunderstood planet. We hope that you will contribute by following the link below.

Please note that the creation of an instant 24 hour poem requires that you WRITE FAST. A new writing challenge will be issued each hour. There is no time for revision, for second thoughts, or for worrying about whose feelings you might hurt. WRITE ON! And while you're here, please enjoy some of the original poems, MP3's and videos we are presenting as part of the party experience. Like a tibetan mandala, this will all be swept away the moment it is complete.
