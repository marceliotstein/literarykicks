LitKicks Presents ...

31 Days. 31 Questions. 31 Chances to tell us what you think.

OCTOBER EARTH was a month-long group writing project that took place on LitKicks.com. Each day in October 2004, we presented a provocative topic for discussion, accompanied by a quote or excerpt from a well-known (or should-be well-known) author or poet. We invited participants to respond with their own thoughts, ideas or further questions. Please enter here to review the entire project.


THE CONCEPT

Our world is torn by ideological conflict. In America, two different news networks often deliver wildly different versions of each day's events. Around the world, people of different nations, religions or cultural backgrounds face off against each other in hatred and suspicion.

OCTOBER EARTH is a group exercise for a divided world. Each day will be a chance to confront our own ideas and ideals. The daily exercises will ask some tough questions, but we are not interested in emphasizing the ways we all disagree. Instead, we want to discover what we all feel together -- what we all love, what we all hate, what we all wish for. To the extent that OCTOBER EARTH is a debate, we want it to be a "sympathetic debate". We want to hear your beliefs, but we want you to hear everybody else's beliefs as well. The only way to win this debate is to change your own mind about something.

Along with the harder questions, there will also be some lighter moments. OCTOBER EARTH is designed to be a valuable learning experience, and we think it will be a good time too.

JOIN IN

The main way to participate is to show up on LitKicks.com between October 1 and October 31. One new discussion topic will be posted each day. You are invited to respond to any or all of the topics.

If you'd like to help in the preparations for the event, you could also recommend a literary quotation or excerpt for us to present as daily conversation-starters. Our aim is to represent a wide range of literary styles, backgrounds, time periods and levels of fame.

If you can know of an interesting piece of writing that fits our concept (see box on left), please send us a note.

Finally, the most important way you can help is by emailing the URL of this page to anybody who you think might be interested. We want to reach every country and city in the world. If you can help us reach people who might enjoy participating, please do.
