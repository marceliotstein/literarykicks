LitKicks Presents ...
     		 

The Freestyle Writing Workshop is an engaging online experience for writers, poets, playwrights and essayists wishing to improve their skills and receive thoughtful feedback. The next session will begin Monday, March 1, 2004 and end on Friday, April 9, 2004.

Here's how it works: once you sign up, you arrive at a group page where you will interact with between 7 and 10 other writers. A writing assignment will be given every two weeks. These assignments are designed to help you break through writer's block and try new creative approaches and techniques. We will ask you to take risks with your work, to write fast, and to aim for power and passion rather than polish and perfection.

The participants will respond to each other with helpful reviews and critiques. All writers must critique, and all critics must write! The environment is designed to be uniquely collaborative, friendly and open-minded.

If you would like to be part of this process, please sign up before the next workshop kicks off (the last day to sign up is February 29). The cost is $20. Anybody who enjoys reading and writing is welcome to join, and no experience or credentials are required. If you'd like more information, check our Frequently Asked Questions page.
