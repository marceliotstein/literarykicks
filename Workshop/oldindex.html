Literary Kicks
 
 
The Freestyle Writing Workshop is an engaging online experience for writers, poets, playwrights and essayists wishing to improve their skills and receive thoughtful feedback. The most recent session began November 17, 2003 and ended on Wednesday, December 31, 2003. If you are enrolled, enter here.

The next session will begin in mid-February 2004, and signups will begin in late January. 		 

We are proud to announce the six winners and five honorable mentions who emerged from The QUEST, an experimental literary workshop/tournament that ran during October 2003. One hundred and fourteen writers participated in The Quest and we'd like to thank them all for their amazing work and great spirit. You can read the work by the six winners, the five honorable mentions and a few other QUEST writers on this page: Morning Talk Belief: Selections From The QUEST, or get to know the winning writers.

If you were a participant in the October 2003 QUEST, you can still view your group's work by signing in here.

We are now finalizing plans for the Summer 2004 publication of a LitKicks anthology that will feature work by the six Quest winners, the five honorable mentions and other LitKicks writers. Stay tuned for more news about this book, and about future QUESTS!
