ginsberg ...

A poem by T. H. Persinger 1994
tpersin@thpgraphics.com

seeing allen ginsberg
rotting ginsberg
beat legend ginsberg
i wonder daily
if when i wake ginsberg will be dead ?
if he is dead when will i first hear of the passing of soul
allen ginsberg,
where are you now ?
allen ginsberg,
are you really living in an alley
with dead mother, meeting father,
not hungry or lonely
without kerouac or cassady ?
allen ginsberg,
I wonder,
allen ginsberg-
sunflower words of Blake and Whitman and french poets
mixed into giant revolutionary breath gasp mile long verse Howl.
allen ginsberg,
1960 song of america
You were afoot and light hearted
as you searched the way-
Are you still ?

Ginsberg, as you leave radioactive planet earth
what do you think ?
what is your elegy of land crippled under weight ?
What ginsberg, what ?

When you were young once, did you imagine
your life would amount to a couple cloth
bound volumes, influence refuted by masses,
excepted disciples, and you guru of modern poetry.

Goodbye Ginsberg.
