Beat Topics: Jazz

I write most of this 'Literary Kicks' stuff myself. But I don't know much about jazz (I have a Pat Metheny album, does that count?), so I think you'll be in better hands reading Mike Janssen's paper on Beats And Jazz.

Or, you could read an article called 'Jazz and the Beat Generation: The Musical Model in Literature', or try a poem called 'That Classic Jazz,' which David Milanaik sent me, or check the pages Sean Daniel Singer wrote me about jazz-influenced Beat poets Ted Joans and Bob Kaufman.

Or we could just let old Jack tell us about it himself ...
