Poetry Happenings

Every once in a while the nice folks at LitKicks get inspired to arrange a poetry reading. This happens at irregular intervals and is usually preceded by the appearance of a comet over North America, or any of several other mysterious phenomena including changes to the Taco Bell menu, sudden drops in world temperature or new Mariah Carey movies.

Once the cosmic signal is caught and the event is triggered, we usually go a little crazy and enter a Dionysian frenzy, subdued but pronounced, that often does not subside until the last incomprehensible poetic verses are shouted into the battered microphone as the sun rises outside the front door of whatever venue we have chosen to invade.

Please join us at the next event, which at his point has not been triggered.

Some of our notable past excesses include ...

  • The AlienFlower LitKicks August Poetry Happening

  • The LitKicks Spring Peace Poetry Happening

  • Poetry & Cornflakes: the first LitKicks event outside New York City took place in Battle Creek, Michigan

  • The LitKicks Summer Poetry Happening and 5th Anniversary Party

  • Valentines Day @ Biblios

We like to feature a mix of well-known poets (we can usually find a few scouting for quarters around the curbs and phone booths of the East Village), Beat legends and LitKicks Action Poets. We also like to smoke too much, drink too much and eat pancakes in the morning.
