Andrew Burnett

Excerpts from Andrew Burnett's fiction, poetry and essays are published on-line in nolo, Basilisk, Litkicks and Gruene Street and off-line in Articulate, Hipink, Red Wheelbarrow, Poeisis and Sideview. A collection of short stories, Zulu Box Tales, is available from Pecan Grove Press, St. Mary's University.

He can be reached via e-mail at aeburnett@hotmail.com.

He created the Neal's Denver mini-site for Literary Kicks, and his work is also available on the web here and here.
