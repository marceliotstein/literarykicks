W. Luther Jett

W. Luther Jett has spent the past twenty-odd years in search of an answer to the question: "Why?" or more properly: "Who?".

He has had variations on this question published in Salon, tomorrow magazine, Liberty, Syncopated City, The Robin's Nest, and Sublime Odyssey, with further variations forthcoming in Synaesthetic andTales of the Stranger's Cafe. His work has also appeared in Poetry Ink, an electronic literary magazine, and Afternoon, a journal published on the World Wide Web.

He has contributed pages on d.a. levy to Literary Kicks.
