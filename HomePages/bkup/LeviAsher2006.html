brooklyn


Full Name: Levi Asher

Title: Chief Executive, Creative Director and Philosopher King, LitKicks.com. In the real world, I also make a living as a Java/PHP techie specializing in data architecture, search engine development and content management systems.

Where I Live: Queens, New York

Writing & Publishing Credits: Action Poetry, an anthology (Literary Kicks, 2004), Tiger's Milk (a poetry chapbook, 2003), Summer of the Mets, a novel (Literary Kicks, 2002). I also directed a primitive digital video, Notes From Underground in 1988, and I guess I've published a lot of random poems, articles and stories in various places I've long ago lost track of.

Writers and books that have influenced me: Well, actually, I spend a lot of time writing about writers that have influenced me, such as: Orhan Pamuk, Jonathan Ames, Jay McInerney, Don DeLillo, John O'Hara, David Foster Wallace, Arthur Conan Doyle, John Fowles, Harold Pinter, Michael McClure, Bret Easton Ellis, Richard Hell, Charles Frazier, Franz Kafka, John Updike, Ann Beattie, T. S. Eliot (a two parter), Arthur Miller, Shakespeare, Jean-Paul Sartre, Paul Verlaine, Henry David Thoreau, Ralph Waldo Emerson, Richard Brautigan, Jack Kerouac and Allen Ginsberg. I think I read too much.
