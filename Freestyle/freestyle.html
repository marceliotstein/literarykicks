LitKicks Presents...
    
LitKicks is pleased to announce the launch of a new, permanent online writing group, the Freestyle Writing Workshop. This project originated with The QUEST, a combination workshop/tournament in which 114 writers competed for six top spots. The Freestyle Writing Workshop will present a continuing version of this experience, but in a slower-moving and less competitive framework. For more information, please click here.

The Freestyle Writing Workshop costs $15/month. For more information about payment and enrollment please visit our Sign Up page.
